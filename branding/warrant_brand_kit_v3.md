# warrant — Brand System v3 (Editorial Monochrome)

## Core Principle

The brand is fundamentally monochrome.

**Primary brand colour:** White `#FFFFFF`

**Secondary brand colour:** Black `#111111`

Pastel colours exist only to communicate meaning. They are never used as core branding.

---

# Brand Positioning

**warrant** is an operating system for coordinating humans and AI agents. It routes work to the best worker, measures outcomes, and ensures irreversible actions always require human approval.

---

# Brand Personality

- Precise
- Calm
- Trustworthy
- Deliberate
- Human
- Intelligent

---

# Visual Direction

Think of an editorial publication rather than enterprise software.

The interface should feel quiet, spacious, and confident.

---

# Typography

- H1 — Semibold

- H3 — Medium

- Caption — Regular

# Pastel Highlights

These colours should occupy less than 5% of any screen.

Sky: `#BFD7FF`

Mint: `#CDEFD6`

Lavender: `#DDD3FF`

Butter: `#FFF3BF`

Peach: `#FFDCCB`

Blush: `#F8D7E6`

## Usage Rules

Sky — focus, selection, information

Mint — trusted human states, approval, success

Butter — warnings

Blush — destructive actions

Lavender & Peach — charts and visual grouping only

---

# Components

Cards: White background, 1px border, no shadows.

Primary Button: Black fill, white text.

Secondary Button: White fill, black border.

Ghost Button: Transparent.

Inputs: White with subtle border and Sky focus ring.

Sidebar: White with pastel active indicator.

---

# Motion

120ms / 180ms / 240ms

Animate only opacity, transform and colour.

Never use bounce or dramatic transitions.

---

# Voice

Clear.

Measured.

Evidence-first.

Never use hype or exaggerated marketing language.
